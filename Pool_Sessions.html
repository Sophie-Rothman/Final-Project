Pool Sessions

We use pool sessions for improving our kayaking technique away from the whitewater, as well as for building community and having fun!

We have pool sessions every Tuesday and Thursday from 9:30 to 11:30 pm and Sundays from 9 to 11 pm in the Dodge Pool.

Come play kayak tag, learn all sorts of rolls and boat tricks, and hang out in our very own hot tub.
