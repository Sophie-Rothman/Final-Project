Welcome to CUWK

Columbia University Whitewater Kayaking is a club devoted to pushing its members out of their comfort zones and challenging them to strive higher, both on and off campus.

We lead weekly trips in the fall and late spring to go kayaking on class I-IV rapids in New York State, Connecticut, Massachusettes, Pennsylvania, and occassionally, beyond.

Additionally, we endeavor to foster a supportive and adventerous community on campus which seeks a better understanding of the outdoors and ourselves.
